Overview

Collaborated with Deloitte's NVIDIA Alliance and Smart Factory team to architect a hybrid Physical AI system for next-generation manufacturing. Fine-tuned Vision-Language and Vision-Language-Action models on NVIDIA Cosmos on real ABB robotic arm data, enabling zero-shot scene understanding and autonomous task execution.

Key Learnings

VLMs enable zero-shot scene understanding without task-specific labeled datasets. World Foundation Models dramatically reduce the sim-to-real transfer gap. Fine-tuning VLA on domain-specific robotic trajectories with as few as 50 demonstrations achieves production-quality control policies.
